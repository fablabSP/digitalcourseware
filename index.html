Digital Courseware Platform

A coordination tool between lecturers, library admin, and book vendors for managing projected and verified digital courseware license counts each financial year (Apr–Apr). All data on this site is hardcoded for demonstration — nothing here is connected to a real database or email system.

L

Lecturer Portal

Submit and verify book license requests for your modules, by financial year.
A

Admin Console

Review requests, manage the book catalogue, vendors, activation checks, and access.
V

Vendor Portal

Placeholder shell — upload catalogues and activation status.

The procurement flow this models

Lecturer discusses needs with a vendor and shares the module code → vendor sends the curation team a pricelist and book details → curation team asks the lecturer to project license counts → library team costs it at the vendor's price plus a 5% markup on the projected amount → that costing goes up for procurement approval → books are purchased → three weeks later the lecturer confirms the final license count needed → both the vendor and the lecturer upload their respective verification lists, which the library team cross-checks for a final reconciliation.

Built as a clickable mockup. See README.md in the repo for the technical spec this will become.
